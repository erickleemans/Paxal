Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose Apache over other web servers?

Apache offers unmatched flexibility, extensive documentation, broad platform support, and a vast ecosystem of modules. Its proven reliability and our expertise make it an excellent choice for businesses of all sizes.

How do you ensure optimal Apache performance?

We implement multi-layered optimization strategies including proper MPM selection, module optimization, caching configuration, and regular performance monitoring. Our team fine-tunes configurations based on your specific traffic patterns and requirements.

What security measures do you implement for Apache servers?

We employ comprehensive security measures including ModSecurity WAF, regular security audits, SSL/TLS implementation, directory access controls, and proper file permissions. We also maintain up-to-date security patches and monitor for potential threats.

Can you handle high-traffic websites?

Yes, we implement load balancing, caching strategies, and performance optimizations specifically designed for high-traffic scenarios. Our configurations can scale to handle millions of requests while maintaining optimal performance.

How do you handle SSL certificate management?

We manage the entire SSL lifecycle, including certificate procurement, installation, configuration, and renewal. We support various SSL providers and can implement Let's Encrypt for automated certificate management.
